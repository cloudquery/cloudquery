Following the launch of [CloudQuery Hub](https://docs.cloudquery.io/blog/announcing-cloudquery-new-hub) last month and premium plugins,
We are excited to announce that we are opening registration for early access for 3rd party developers who are interested in 
developing and selling plugins, transformations and visaulizations on CloudQuery Hub.

## Key Details

- Only Go plugins are currently supported for 3rd party developers to sell on CQ Hub (
  We will support eventually all other CQ SDK languages such as Python/Java/Javascript)
- Plugins are charged in a usage based pricing with $X per 1M rows per month that the developer sets.
- Transformations and visualizations are charged as one time fee per 6 months (and will include support and updates for those 6 months).
- CloudQuery takes care of billing, charing, usage and metering and charges 30% comission out of each sale.
- **Early Registration discount**: Anyone who is registring till end of Q1 2024 will be able to redeem 15% discount on comission till end of 2024.

Registration is now open [here](https://docs.cloudquery.io/register-for-marketplace)! 

Also, as always feel free to hop on our [discord](https://cloudquery.io/discord) for more questions!
