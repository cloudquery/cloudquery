Hey everyone! Our monthly round-up of CloudQuery product and roadmap updates is here! This month was packed!

## New Integrations Page

Check out our new [integrations page](https://www.cloudquery.io/integrations)!

## SDK Updates

- Better [migration approach](https://github.com/cloudquery/plugin-sdk/pull/688) that allows users to choose between `safe` and `forced` migrations for all destinations. `safe` is the default for all destinations, and `forced` is supported for ClickHouse, MySQL, PostgreSQL and SQLite at the moment (we'll add others gradually).
